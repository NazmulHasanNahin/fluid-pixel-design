

# Awake-Style Design Agency Website

A single-page agency website with rich animations, clean UI, and modern design matching the reference screenshots.

## Sections to Build

1. **Navbar** — Sticky header with logo ("Awake" + diamond icon), pill-shaped nav links (Home, About us, Services, Work, Team, Pricing, Awards), and a dark "Let's Collaborate" CTA button with arrow icon. Active link gets a subtle highlight.

2. **Hero Section** — Large serif/italic headline "Building bold brands with *thoughtful design*" (italic for emphasis). Subtitle text below. "Get Started" CTA button. Social proof row: overlapping avatar circles, 4.5-star rating, "Trusted by 1000+ clients". Subtle fade-in and slide-up animations on load.

3. **Logo Marquee** — Horizontal scrolling ticker of partner logos with "Loved by 1000+ big and small brands" label. Continuous smooth scroll animation.

4. **About/Stats Section** — Heading: "Crafting exceptional, well experienced & technology driven strategies..." with colorful pill badges (Creativity, Innovation, Strategy) in pastel colors. Three large stat counters (40+ Projects, 15+ Years, 12+ Awards) with count-up animation on scroll.

5. **Services Section** — "Where innovation meets *aesthetics*" heading. Five service cards in a row with pastel backgrounds (purple, pink, blue, peach, green), each with an icon and label (Brand Strategy, Web Development, Digital Marketing, UI/UX Design, Analytics). Hover scale effect.

6. **CTA Banner** — Dark rounded banner: "See Our Work in Action. Start Your Creative Journey with Us!" with two buttons (Let's Collaborate, View Portfolio).

7. **Portfolio/Work Section** — "business's *online presence*" heading. 2x2 grid of project cards with large images, project name, and category tags (UX Research, Interface Design, etc.). Hover zoom effect on images.

8. **Testimonials Section** — "what clients are saying *about us*" heading. Bento-grid layout: large testimonial card with dark overlay on image + quote, yellow "Facts & numbers" card (91% stat), smaller testimonial cards. Staggered fade-in animations.

9. **Pricing Section** — "Pick the plan that fits your *start-up*" heading. Two pricing cards side by side — Starter ($2,500/mo, yellow bg) and Pro ($3,800/mo, purple/blue bg with white text). Feature checklists. CTA buttons on each.

10. **FAQ Section** — "Got questions? We've got *answers*" heading with accordion-style expandable questions.

11. **Final CTA Section** — Gradient background (light blue to peach). "Innovative solutions for *bold brands*" heading with description and "Let's craft together" button.

12. **Footer** — Logo + tagline, social icons (X, LinkedIn, Dribbble, Instagram), sitemap links, other pages, contact details (address, email, phone). Copyright line.

## Design & Animation Details

- **Typography**: Mix of clean sans-serif for body and elegant serif/script italic for accent words
- **Color palette**: Mostly black & white with yellow, purple, pastel accents
- **Animations**: Scroll-triggered fade-ins, count-up numbers, hover scales on cards, smooth marquee, staggered reveals for grid items
- **Responsive**: Mobile-friendly with stacked layouts on smaller screens

## Technical Approach
- Single-page layout with smooth scroll navigation
- Intersection Observer for scroll-triggered animations
- CSS keyframes for marquee and entrance animations
- All content is static/hardcoded (no backend needed)
